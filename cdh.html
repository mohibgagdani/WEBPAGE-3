CharDham Hotels

In Hindu mythology, Char Dham represents a symbolic pilgrimage aimed at attaining moksha. The term "Char Dham" is derived from the Hindi words "Char" and "Dham," signifying four destinations. These four sacred sites include Gangotri, the origin of the holy Ganga River, Yamunotri, the source of the Yamuna River, and the two revered shrines, Kedarnath and Badrinath. They are regarded as the path to spiritual enlightenment and eternal salvation. Keeping the accommodation for the devotees of Char Dham Yatra in priority, we provide the best options for hotels and lodges that come with an easy booking procedure. Several ashrams and dharamshalas also provide reasonable accommodation options to the pilgrims. Most of the private hotels, lodges, and guest houses cater to the basic requirements with primary amenities and facilities. High-end luxury hotels are also available at certain locations en route to the Char Dham routes. Kindly note that all types of accommodations of all budgets and ranges provide only vegetarian food and might not have an array of cuisines. The peak season of Char Dham Yatra records heavy footfall of devotees. Therefore it is advisable to conduct prior bookings of hotels or other accommodations for a smooth experience of the pilgrimage.
